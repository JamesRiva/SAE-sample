Company Overview

Trusted by the Philippines'
Leading Industries

SAE Products Marketing Corporation has been a trusted partner for some of the country's leading manufacturing and processing companies, including many of the top 600 corporations. Established in 1980, SAE is the first Filipino company to engage in the supply, service, and distribution of advanced energy-saving systems designed to help clients reduce electrical consumption, maintenance costs, emissions, and operational inefficiencies.

Proudly 100% Filipino-owned, SAE has earned its position as an industry pioneer, bringing advanced technology and expertise to businesses across the Philippines. This commitment not only supports the growth of businesses but also contributes to a greener, more sustainable future for the nation.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped
an Industry

1980

Foundation

SAE Products Marketing Corporation established as the first Filipino company in energy-saving systems distribution and service.

1983

Local Assembly Pioneer

First Filipino company to locally assemble rotary screw compressors with imported air ends from the UK and motors from Brazil, making advanced technology accessible to mid-sized clients.

1994

Centrifugal Compressor Leadership

Extensively introduced centrifugal compressors to semiconductors, electronics, beverages, and air separation sectors — achieving over 120 installations of Ingersoll Rand centrifugal compressors.

Now

Sustainable Future

Continuing to lead as the technology partner for decarbonization through energy efficiency, enhanced productivity, lower emissions, and reduced maintenance costs.
